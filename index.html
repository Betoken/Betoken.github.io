The control over the fund's assets is denominated by Kairo tokens. During every investment cycle, each participant stakes Kairo tokens to make investment proposals for the fund (e.g. 'Buy 10 OMG tokens'), which are subsequently executed.

If a proposal is proved profitable in the investment cycle, the staker wins Kairo tokens relative to the profit made.

Over time, the control over fund assets flows to the best investors, a win-win for both the performance of the fund as well as the fund managers. Moreover, a small proportion of the fund is redistributed to Kairo token holders, providing an additional value to the token.

Smart Contract Security

All funds are stored in the Betoken contract, eliminating counterparty risk and allowing fast, automated Kairo transfers to winning forecasters. Pre-specified parameters mean you don't need to worry about hidden clauses or extra fees.

Investment proposals are automatically turned into trade orders and placed onto decentralized exchanges. Depositing and withdrawing funds are also completely automated using the Ethereum network.
