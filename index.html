Here's How It Works:

The control over the fund's assets is denominated by Kairo tokens. During every investment cycle, each participant stakes Kairo tokens to make investment proposals for the fund (e.g. 'Buy 10 BAT tokens'), which are subsequently executed. If a proposal is proved profitable at the end of the investment cycle, those who staked in support of it wins the Kairo tokens staked by the opposing side, and vise versa.

Over time, the control over fund assets flows to the best investors, a win-win for both the performance of the fund as well as the fund managers. Moreover, a small proportion of the fund is redistributed to Kairo token holders, providing an additional value to the token.
